About Me

Hello! My name is Kendyl Eugenio, and I am a senior at Gonzaga University pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology with a minor in General Business. I proudly identify as a second-generation Filipina-American and was born and raised in Ewa Beach, Hawaii. My upbringing in such a tight-knit community fostered early exposure to people from diverse backgrounds, shaping my values and fueling my passion for social justice. Driven by these values, I am deeply committed to advocating for educational, health, and workplace equity. I am an enthusiastic and driven individual with personal and professional goals that consist of understanding human behavior and business to improve the health and well-being of people within my community.
